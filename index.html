🛂 Indian Visa Information

E-Visa (Recommended for most countries)

  • Processing Time: 2-4 business days
  • Validity: 30 or 60 days from issue
  • Cost: $10-25 USD (varies by country)
  • Multiple Entry: Available
  • Application: Online only

How to Apply:

  1. Visit: indianvisaonline.gov.in
  2. Fill online form (10 minutes)
  3. Upload documents (passport scan, photo)
  4. Pay visa fee online
  5. Receive approval via email (2-4 days)
  6. Print approval letter

Required Documents:

  • ✓ Valid Passport (6+ months validity)
  • ✓ Passport scan (bio page)
  • ✓ Recent photo (digital, white background)
  • ✓ Email address
  • ✓ Credit/Debit card for payment

Travel Insurance:

Recommended to get travel insurance covering:

  • ✓ Flight cancellations
  • ✓ Lost luggage
  • ✓ Trip delays

Important Notes:

  • • E-Visa is valid for entry at designated airports only
